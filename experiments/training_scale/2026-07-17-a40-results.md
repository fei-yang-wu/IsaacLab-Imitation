# A40 latent-training scale result (2026-07-17)

## Recommendation

For the shortest time to a sustained episodic return of 7.5 on one A40, use:

```text
num_envs=8192
agent.collector.frames_per_batch=12
agent.loss.mini_batch_size=12288
agent.loss.epochs=5
```

This gives a 98,304-sample global PPO rollout, eight minibatches per epoch,
and 40 optimizer steps per rollout. Across seeds 0, 1, and 2 it confirmed the
target after a median 50.04M frames and 1,441.79 seconds (24m02s) of training.

The throughput-oriented alternative is 12,288 environments, 8 rollout steps,
and a 24,576 minibatch. It has a much higher median frame rate, but needs a
median 60.06M frames and confirms in 1,450.34 seconds (24m10s). The eight-second
median timing difference is not practically meaningful; the recommended
8,192-environment setting wins on samples to target, scene-creation cost,
memory headroom, rollout horizon, and median time. Use 12,288 x 8 instead when
training well beyond the 7.5 target: its median full-75M time is 1,782.59
seconds versus 2,168.79 seconds for the optimizer-heavy setting.
